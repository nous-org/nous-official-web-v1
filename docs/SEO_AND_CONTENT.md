# SEO And Content Governance

This document defines the SEO and content rules that should remain consistent across the NOUS website.

## Positioning

NOUS should be presented as an AI transformation agency for organizations in Costa Rica and LatAm.

Preferred language:

- AI transformation
- intelligence deployment
- automation
- AI systems
- adoption
- operational leverage
- Costa Rica and LatAm

Avoid drifting back into generic "web development agency" positioning unless a page is specifically about web systems as part of AI-ready infrastructure.

## URL Strategy

| Content Type | English | Spanish |
|---|---|---|
| Homepage | `/` | `/es` |
| Services | `/services` | `/es/services` |
| About | `/about` | `/es/about` |
| Contact | `/contact` | `/es/contact` |
| Blog | `/blog` | `/es/blog` |
| Privacy | `/privacy-policy` | `/es/privacy-policy` |
| Terms | `/terms-and-conditions` | `/es/terms-and-conditions` |

Rules:

- Use slashless canonical URLs.
- Use lowercase paths.
- Use permanent redirects for retired URLs.
- Do not create duplicate routes for the same intent.
- Do not reintroduce `/pricing` or `/products` unless the business strategy changes.

## Metadata Requirements

Every indexable page should have:

- descriptive title tag
- meta description with clear value proposition
- self-referencing canonical URL
- Open Graph title, description, image, and URL
- Twitter card metadata
- appropriate hreflang alternates when an equivalent Spanish/English page exists

Title guidance:

```text
[Primary Topic] in Costa Rica & LatAm | NOUS
[Service/Use Case] | NOUS
[Article Title] / NOUS
```

Meta description guidance:

- keep it specific to the page
- mention AI transformation, automation, deployment, or intelligent systems where relevant
- include Costa Rica/LatAm context when it helps search intent
- avoid repeating the title verbatim

## Structured Data

Use JSON-LD components for:

- Organization
- WebSite
- Service
- Article or BlogPosting
- FAQPage when visible FAQs exist
- BreadcrumbList where breadcrumbs are visible or route hierarchy is useful

Schema must match visible page content. Do not add review, rating, price, or FAQ markup unless that content is visible and compliant.

## Indexation Rules

Indexable:

- homepage
- services
- about
- contact
- legal pages
- published blog posts
- blog index

Noindex or exclude:

- `/admin`
- `/portfolio` while it is not a mature public case-study section
- `404`
- API routes
- retired redirects
- local/generated artifacts

The sitemap is generated by Astro. Do not commit a manual `public/sitemap.xml`.

## Redirect Rules

Current retired routes:

```text
/about-us    -> /about
/contact-us  -> /contact
/pricing     -> /services
/products    -> /services
/es/about-us -> /es/about
/es/contact-us -> /es/contact
/es/pricing  -> /es/services
/es/products -> /es/services
```

When adding redirects:

- use `301` for permanent replacements
- keep the target intent-equivalent
- update `public/_redirects`
- update `src/middleware.ts`
- add or update static QA tests

## Content Quality

Priority pages should answer:

- what NOUS does
- who the service is for
- what problem it solves
- how the engagement works
- what outcomes a buyer should expect
- how to take the next step

For service pages, include:

- use cases
- process
- expected business outcomes
- implementation approach
- trust or proof points
- internal links to contact and relevant articles

For blog posts, include:

- clear article intent
- date and author information where available
- internal links to relevant service pages
- structured summaries or sections
- practical examples when possible

## Language Quality

English and Spanish pages should be localized, not mechanically mirrored. Keep the same intent and conversion path, but adapt phrasing for the audience.

Spanish should be natural for Costa Rica/LatAm business readers and should avoid untranslated English boilerplate unless it is a product or technical term users expect.

## AI Search Readiness

Content should make entity relationships explicit:

- NOUS is the organization.
- NOUS provides AI transformation, automation, and intelligent systems services.
- NOUS serves Costa Rica and LatAm.
- Contact paths and service definitions should be clear in visible copy and schema.

Prefer concise definitions, clear service names, FAQs, and examples that answer real buyer questions.
